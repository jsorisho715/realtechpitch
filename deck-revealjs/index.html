05-founder-market-fit.md

The SmartRent question. Honest risk

5 of 6 of us are current SmartRent leadership (3 VPs + 2 Directors). Path A's strongest bet (Channel Partner Portal) sits closest to SmartRent's surface. Per the SmartRent Employee Handbook (Oct 2025), here's what we owe each other to decide openly.

Three handbook clauses that apply
  • §1.6 Conflicts of Interest — being employed by, consulting for, or owning interest in "a competitor or potential competitor, supplier, contractor, client, or customer."
  • §1.10 Outside Employment — "outside employment that competes with [or] constitutes a conflict of interest with or creates an adverse impact on SmartRent is prohibited."
  • §3.5 Corporate Opportunities — "no Employee may compete with the Company"; cannot take opportunities discovered through SmartRent position unless approved by Legal Department.
Apps × Risk read
  • Channel Partner Portal — High. Multifamily operator-vendor portal lives close to SmartRent's "manage daily operations processes" surface. Customer-overlap real.
  • Real Tech GTM, Rovian, Wedding, Kith, edgenote, Baby, DealPayment, Cannabis, Rehabit, Life Events Suite — Low. Different markets, different buyers, no SmartRent overlap.
  • Real Tech Consulting (parent) — Disclose. Outside work, generally allowed under §1.10 with manager + HR notice; do not solicit SmartRent customers.
Four disposition paths for Channel Partner Portal

ADrop it

Most conservative. Removes risk entirely. Loses the once-in-a-decade founder-fit.

BDefer to post-departure

The 5 SmartRent founders only build / sell after they've left. Tim alone is independent — but can't carry the build alone.

CGet Legal Dept approval

§3.5 carve-out: written authorization from SmartRent Legal. Required if any of the 4 are involved before leaving.

DRe-scope away from overlap

Pivot to commercial real estate, hospitality chains, or HOAs — sectors SmartRent doesn't serve. Lower founder-fit, lower risk.

What we agree today
  • Until disposition is chosen, no SmartRent customer relationships are activated for Channel Partner Portal pilots.
  • Tim is the only founder fully independent of SmartRent — he can do concept / market / competitor research without triggering §1.6. Halim is now also bound; he can build code on personal time but not for the Channel Portal until disposition is settled.
  • Mutual NDA's third-party IP protection clause (§7) already prevents any current-employer info from being shared into Real Tech.
  • If we pick Path A (GTM + Channel Portal) on the recommendation slide, we also pick a disposition A / B / C / D for Channel Portal.
