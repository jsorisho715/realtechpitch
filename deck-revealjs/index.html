Outside-employment review. Internal · cofounder eyes only

5 of 6 of us currently lead at a multifamily-proptech employer. Path A's strongest bet (Channel Partner Portal) sits closest to that employer's surface. We name the risk now and decide openly. Named handbook citations in the supporting doc.

Three handbook clauses that apply (current employer)
  • §1.6 Conflicts of Interest — being employed by, consulting for, or owning interest in "a competitor or potential competitor, supplier, contractor, client, or customer."
  • §1.10 Outside Employment — "outside employment that competes with [or] constitutes a conflict of interest with or creates an adverse impact on [the Company] is prohibited."
  • §3.5 Corporate Opportunities — "no Employee may compete with the Company"; cannot take opportunities discovered through Company position unless approved by Legal Department.
Apps × Risk read
  • Channel Partner Portal — High. Multifamily operator-vendor portal lives close to the current employer's "manage daily operations processes" surface. Customer-overlap real.
  • Real Tech GTM, Rovian, Wedding, Kith, edgenote, Baby, DealPayment, Cannabis, Rehabit, Life Events Suite — Low. Different markets, different buyers, no overlap with current employer's product.
  • Real Tech Consulting (parent) — Disclose. Outside work, generally allowed under §1.10 with manager + HR notice; do not solicit current-employer customers.
Four disposition paths for Channel Partner Portal

ADrop it

Most conservative. Removes risk entirely. Loses the once-in-a-decade founder-fit.

BDefer to post-departure

The 5 currently-employed founders only build / sell after they've left. Tim alone is independent — but can't carry the build alone.

CGet Legal Dept approval

§3.5 carve-out: written authorization from the current employer's Legal Department. Required if any of the 5 are involved before leaving.

DRe-scope away from overlap

Pivot to commercial real estate, hospitality chains, or HOAs — sectors the current employer doesn't serve. Lower founder-fit, lower risk.

What we agree today
  • Until disposition is chosen, no current-employer customer relationships are activated for Channel Partner Portal pilots.
  • Tim is the only founder fully independent of the current employer — he can do concept / market / competitor research without triggering §1.6. Halim is also bound; he can build code on personal time but not for the Channel Portal until disposition is settled.
  • Mutual NDA's third-party IP protection clause (§7) already prevents any current-employer info from being shared into Real Tech.
  • If we pick Path A (GTM + Channel Portal) on the recommendation slide, we also pick a disposition A / B / C / D for Channel Portal.
