# 03 — Decision Framework

The rubric the cofounders will vote with. Math is sound (weights sum to 100). Scoring is 1-10 per criterion. Apply to all 10 apps + Life Events Suite bundle.

---

## Why a Rubric

Without a shared scoring framework, the meeting becomes "loudest opinion wins." With one, the data-first founders feel respected, the relationship-first founders see their partner pipelines counted, and disagreement happens at the **weight** level rather than the **outcome** level.

This rubric is **debatable**. Cofounders can adjust the weights live during the meeting if they disagree. The point is *transparency*, not *correctness*.

---

## Scoring Criteria & Weights

Total weight = 100.

| Criterion | Weight | What it measures | Why it matters |
|-----------|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **Build Completeness** | 15 | % of code/product already shipped | Built things ship; concepts slip |
| **Market Readiness** | 10 | Product-market fit signal: paying users, demos converting, market timing | Hot category × ready buyer = momentum |
| **GTM Clarity** | 12 | Do we know who buys, how to reach them, what they pay? | Clear ICP and channel = predictable growth |
| **Channel Readiness** | 15 | Named warm partners or pipeline available TODAY | This is where context matters: multifamily operator network, Cannabis partners, dealership pipeline |
| **Founder-Market Fit** | 18 | Does this team have unique advantages (network, expertise, prior success) | The single biggest predictor of startup success per first-principles VC analysis |
| **Moat / Defensibility** | 10 | Patent, network effect, data, switching cost, brand | Determines whether we keep what we earn |
| **Speed to First $10k MRR** | 10 | Months to first material revenue | Cash flow funds the studio model |
| **Capital Required** | 5 | Inverse — low capital = higher score | Bootstrap-friendly = optionality preserved |
| **Strategic Fit (Real Tech identity)** | 5 | Does this fit the parent brand? Does it strengthen consulting GTM? | Avoids brand confusion |

**Why these weights?** Founder-Market Fit (18) and Channel Readiness (15) get the highest weights because they're the two factors that distinguish *us* from generic founders pursuing the same ideas. Build Completeness (15) is third because shipped products beat slides every time. Capital and Strategic Fit are kept low — they're filters, not drivers.

## Sensitivity Analysis

What happens if we re-weight?

### Scenario A — "Cash flow first" (boost Speed-to-MRR + Capital, drop Founder-Fit)

Increase Speed weight to 20, Capital to 10, drop Founder-Fit to 12, drop Channel to 10.

| Result | App | New rank |
|--------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Real Tech GTM | unchanged |
| 2 | Wedding Planner AI | up |
| 3 | Rovian.ai | unchanged-ish |
| 4 | Kith | up |
| 5 | edgenote.ai | up |

### Scenario B — "Channel-led" (boost Channel + Founder-Fit, drop Build)

Increase Channel to 22, Founder-Fit to 22, drop Build to 8.

| Result | App | New rank |
|--------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Channel Partner Portal | up to #1 |
| 2 | Real Tech GTM | down to #2 |
| 3 | Rovian.ai | unchanged |

### Scenario C — "Moat-first / IP play" (boost Moat + Strategic, drop Speed)

Increase Moat to 18, Strategic to 10, drop Speed to 5, drop Capital to 2.

| Result | App | New rank |
|--------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Real Tech GTM | unchanged |
| 2 | Channel Partner Portal | up |
| 3 | DealPayment | up to #3 (patent moat) |

The point of these scenarios: **the top 3 candidates (GTM, Rovian, Channel Partner / Wedding) are robust across reasonable re-weighting.** Reasonable people can argue for any of them. That's what we vote on.

---

## How the Vote Works in the Meeting

1. **Adjust weights live** if any cofounder disagrees with the defaults. Weights must still sum to 100.
2. **Re-score any app** the team disagrees on (especially Channel Readiness for Cannabis and DealPayment — partners' confidentiality means scoring is from named context, not public data).
3. **Recompute weighted totals** (the deck has a live spreadsheet view in the appendix).
4. **Each cofounder picks their top 2** privately, then we reveal.
5. **Discussion + final vote.**

The two scenarios surfaced on the recommendation slide:

- **"Multifamily-Lane Activation path" (recommended):** Real Tech GTM + Channel Partner Portal
- **"Channel-Activation path":** Rovian + DealPayment

Both score well under different reasonable weights. The team picks based on appetite (cash-flow vs. brand-build).

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid in the Vote

- **"It's my baby"** — don't score your own app artificially high
- **"Halim/Jonathan can build it fast"** — engineering speed shouldn't be the dominant factor; market matters more
- **"What if we did all 10?"** — the studio model fails if focus dilutes; we pick 2 + maintain 1 (Kith) + put 7 on hold
- **"Let's just decide later"** — the value of this meeting is the decision; defer = drift
